Some pre-configured namelist.input files (for cm1r13, released 20 March 2009):

  • Supercell
    • Reference: Weisman and Rotunno, 2000, JAS, p. 1452.
    • Configuration is the 1/4 Circle case.
  • Squall line
    • Reference: Bryan, Wyngaard, and Fritsch, 2003, MWR, p. 2394.
    • Configuration is the 1-km weak-shear case.
  • Axisymmetric hurricane
    • Reference: Bryan and Rotunno, 2009, MWR, in press.
    • Note: this case is configured to use the moist neutral sounding from Rotunno and Emanuel (1987), by default.
    • Configuration is the 1x resolution (RE87) setup.
  • 3D hurricane
    • 3d version of the Bryan and Rotunno (2009) simulations.
    • Configuration is the 1x resolution (RE87) setup.
